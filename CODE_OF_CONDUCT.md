# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, specific, patient, and accountable.
Critique ideas and evidence rather than people.
Respect privacy, security boundaries, and maintainer decisions made transparently for project safety.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, unwanted sexual attention, doxxing, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer contact listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).
Maintainers may edit, remove, reject, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation based on severity and context.
Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project publicly.
